## 🧠 Problem

Saving content on platforms like Instagram is clunky and inefficient.

- Saved reels get buried and are hard to find  
- No real organization (folders are just tags)  
- Slow scrolling and loading  
- No cross-platform management  
- No privacy control  

Users consume a lot of content, but **can’t actually use or retrieve it when needed**.

---

## 💡 Solution

ClipVault is a **personal content library** that lets you capture, organize, and access content effortlessly.

It turns chaotic saved content into a **fast, searchable, structured workspace**.

---

## ⚡ Key Features

- **Share → Auto Save**  
  Save any reel or post in 1–2 taps directly from apps  

- **Bulk Import**  
  Upload Instagram export (JSON/HTML) to import hundreds of saved reels instantly  

- **Automatic Organization**  
  Content is auto-tagged and categorized on import  

- **AI Organize (In Progress)**  
  One tap to intelligently organize complex or uncategorized content  

- **Fast Browsing**  
  Pre-parsed content enables smooth, instant scrolling  

- **Folders & Tags**  
  Full control over organizing your library  

- **Profile Library (In Development)**  
  Follow content without following accounts  

- **Privacy First**  
  No Instagram login required  
  Optional local storage  

---

## 🏗️ How It Works

1. Upload file or share link to ClipVault  
2. It gets automatically parsed and saved  
3. Content is organized and searchable instantly  
4. Browse your library at high speed  

---

## 🎯 Why I Built This

I personally save a lot of content but could never find it when needed.

Existing solutions were slow, manual, and limited.

So I built a working prototype in ~10 minutes to:
- Remove friction  
- Automate organization  
- Create a true personal content workspace
